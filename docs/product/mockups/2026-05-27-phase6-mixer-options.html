Option A

Best for delight and quick discovery.

This makes Wearabouts feel smart immediately after wardrobe upload. It is weaker for precise control unless every suggestion can open into an edit surface.

Option B

Best for control and learning user taste.

This makes lock/swap behavior central and creates better signals for future trip and daily recommendations. It may feel less magical unless the first outfit is already good.
